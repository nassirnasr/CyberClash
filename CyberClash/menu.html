Learn About Game CyberClash

Welcome to CyberClash, a game designed to increase your awareness about online security and phishing attacks.

Game Objective

Protect yourself from cyber threats by defeating the hacker in a card battle. Your goal is to reduce the hacker's life points to zero before your own life points run out.

How to Play

  • Select a card from your deck to play against the hacker.
  • Compare the power of your card with the hacker's card:
    • If your card's power is higher, the hacker loses life points.
    • If the hacker's card has higher power, you lose life points.
    • If powers are equal, it's a tie.
  • Repeat the process until either you or the hacker runs out of life points.

Difficulty Levels

Cyber Clash offers different difficulty settings:

  • Easy: No time restriction. Take your time to choose cards.
  • Medium: 10-second time limit per turn. Quick thinking required!
  • Hard: 5-second time limit per turn. Test your reflexes and decision-making under pressure!
